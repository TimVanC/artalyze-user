1. Data Collection

We collect the following types of data:

  • Account Information: Email, first name, and last name.
  • Game Data: Scores, selections, and streaks.
  • Device Information: Browser type, IP address, and device details.
  • Usage Data: Interaction with the platform, including game activity.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to:

  • Enhance and personalize your Artalyze experience.
  • Track game progress and maintain statistics.
  • Improve website functionality and detect bugs.
  • Ensure compliance with platform rules and prevent abuse.

3. Security Measures

We take data security seriously and implement:

  • Secure authentication and encryption protocols.
  • Regular security audits and updates.
  • Restricted access to personal data.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain data for as long as necessary to provide services. If an account is deleted:

  • All personal data is immediately erased from our systems.
  • Game-related data may be anonymized for analytics purposes.

5. Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of your stored data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Modify or update your account details.

6. Third-Party Services

Artalyze may use third-party services for analytics and performance monitoring. These third parties:

  • Do not have access to personal account details.
  • May collect anonymized usage data for improving services.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this policy as needed. Users will be notified of significant changes.
